Welcome to Indie Dev Toys

A collection of practical tools for indie developers. All tools run entirely in your browser.

Seamless Texture

Convert any image into a seamless, tileable texture with advanced blending options.

Background Remover

Remove unwanted background areas and export with transparency.

Texture Dilation

Expand edge pixels to avoid artifacts in texture atlases and sprite sheets.

Normal Map Generator

Generate high-quality normal maps from any color/diffuse texture.

Channel Packer

Combine multiple textures into RGBA channels for Unity, Unreal, or custom workflows.

Atlas Packer

Combine multiple sprites into a single texture atlas with grid or automatic packing.

Sprite Extractor

Automatically detect and extract individual sprites from a sprite sheet.

Quick Image Adjustment

Batch-adjust hue, saturation, brightness, contrast, resolution, and aspect ratio for multiple textures.

File Converter

Convert images, audio, and video files between popular formats entirely in your browser — no upload required.
